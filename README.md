# Sales Performance & Growth Analytics

The project focused on transforming raw, unverified data into a dynamic dashboard to track regional performance, profitability by category, and Year-over-Year (YoY) growth trends.

# Data Processing
**Cleaning the Data:** I took nearly 10,000 messy rows of sales data and used Excel & Power Query to fix errors and remove duplicates. This created a single, reliable "Master" list to work from.

**Calculating Results:** I built formulas and Pivot Tables to find important numbers like Year-over-Year (YoY) growth and which regions were making the most money.

**Building the Dashboard:** I designed an easy-to-use dashboard with Slicers. This lets anyone filter the data by year, region, or product type with just one click.

# **Dashboard Insights**

**Tracking Success:** The dashboard shows a 20.6% increase in sales in the last year. This proves the business is growing fast, meaning the company should order more stock to keep up with demand.

**Smart Marketing:** I found that California and New York are the biggest profit makers. The company can now save money by spending its marketing budget where it actually gets the best results.

**Better Product Focus:** The data shows that Technology products make the most profit ($145k+), while Furniture makes much less. This tells the company to focus more on selling gadgets and tech to make more money.
